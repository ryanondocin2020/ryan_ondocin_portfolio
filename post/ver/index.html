Blockchain Virtual Escape Room

May 3, 2019

Created a virtual escape room using google slides to teach students about blockhcain. This mystery follows a scandal on what was once the world’s largest illegal drug marketplaces on the dark web. It harnesses real life clues from the FBIs investigation along with interactive quizzes and visualizations to help participants understand core components of blockchain technology. It’s an enthralling adventure from start to finish and is currently being licensed by Syracuse University for educational use in the future. Unearth these clues to apprehend the suspect!
